# AI Tools Documentation

This document provides an overview of the AI tools and software utilized in the tajong project, including their descriptions, pricing, and usage guidance.

## 1. Claude
- **Description:** Claude is an AI language model developed by Anthropic, designed for natural language understanding and generation.
- **Pricing:** Pricing is available upon request from Anthropic.
- **Usage:** Suitable for tasks such as text generation, summarization, and question answering.

## 2. Gemini
- **Description:** Developed by Google DeepMind, Gemini is an advanced AI model that combines various machine learning techniques for improved efficiency and accuracy.
- **Pricing:** Pricing is based on API usage; refer to Google for details.
- **Usage:** Effective for complex problem-solving, data analysis, and more.

## 3. Grок
- **Description:** Grок is a generative AI platform focused on providing AI-driven solutions for creative industries.
- **Pricing:** Subscription-based model; exact pricing can vary based on features.
- **Usage:** Ideal for content generation, media production, and design tasks.

## 4. Canva
- **Description:** Canva is a graphic design platform that uses AI tools to streamline the design process for various projects.
- **Pricing:** Free tier available; Pro plans start at ~$12.95/month.
- **Usage:** Perfect for creating presentations, social media graphics, and other visual content.

## 5. 11Labs
- **Description:** 11Labs specializes in AI-driven voice synthesis and audio enhancement technologies.
- **Pricing:** Pricing is tiered; check their website for current offers.
- **Usage:** Useful for voiceovers, audio production, and language dubbing.

## 6. Google Slides
- **Description:** A part of Google Workspace, Google Slides allows users to create, edit, and collaborate on presentations.
- **Pricing:** Free for individual users; Workspace plans provide additional features.
- **Usage:** Employed for creating engaging presentations and collaborative work.
