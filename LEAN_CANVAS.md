# Lean Canvas for tajong (아시나요)

## Problem
- Identify key problems faced by your customers that the tajong project is addressing.

## Solution
- Outline the main solutions your product or service offers to the problems identified.

## Key Metrics
- Define the critical metrics that will gauge the success of your business model.

## Unique Value Proposition
- Describe what makes your offering unique and why customers should choose it over alternatives.

## Channels
- Identify the pathways through which you will reach your customers.

## Customer Segments
- Define the different groups of people or organizations you aim to reach and serve.

## Cost Structure
- List the main costs involved in operating your business model.

## Revenue Streams
- Outline how your business will earn money through different sources.

## Existing Alternatives
- Identify current alternatives in the market that address the same problems.
